Prepare Your Family and Community for Disasters

Follow these simple steps to ensure safety during natural disasters like floods, earthquakes, tornadoes, and more. Being prepared saves lives.

Get Started
Step 1
Prepare Your Home

Secure furniture, check emergency exits, and keep a disaster supply kit ready with water, food, and first aid.

Step 2
Build an Emergency Kit

Include essentials such as food, water, medications, flashlights, batteries, and important documents in a grab-and-go bag.

Step 3
Create a Family Plan

Establish evacuation routes, meeting points, and emergency contacts. Ensure all family members know the plan.

Step 4
Stay Informed

Sign up for alerts, monitor local news, and follow NDMS social media channels for real-time updates during disasters.

Step 5
Know Emergency Services

Familiarize yourself with local emergency services, hospitals, and shelters. Keep contact numbers handy.

Step 6
Practice Drills

Conduct regular fire, flood, and earthquake drills at home and in your community to be ready for any emergency.
