Prepare Your Family and Community

Follow these steps to ensure safety during natural disasters. Being prepared saves lives.

Get Started
Step 1
Prepare Your Home

Secure furniture, check emergency exits, and keep a disaster supply kit ready.

Step 2
Build an Emergency Kit

Include essentials such as food, water, medications, and important documents.

Step 3
Create a Family Plan

Establish evacuation routes, meeting points, and emergency contacts.

Step 4
Stay Informed

Sign up for alerts, monitor local news, and follow NDMS social media updates.

Step 5
Know Emergency Services

Familiarize yourself with local emergency services, hospitals, and shelters.

Step 6
Practice Drills

Conduct regular fire, flood, and earthquake drills at home and in your community.
